INDIAN FREEDOM MOVEMENT

The Indian freedom movements started in 1857 with the revolt of 1857. The first war of independence against British rule. It began in Meerut against
the East India Company. The indian independence movement was a series of historic event with the ultimate aim of ending british rule
in india also known as british Raj. it lasted until 1947.
